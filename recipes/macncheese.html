Tini's Mac & Cheese

Mac & Cheese made after Tini's viral TikTok Mac & Cheese

Ingredients

  • Cheddar
  • Mozarella
  • Elbow Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Heavy Cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Paprika
  • Mustard Powder

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook macaroni
  2. Melt butter in a pan
  3. Add flour and whisk
  4. Add milk and heavy cream
  5. Add spices
  6. Add cheese
  7. Add macaroni
  8. Enjoy!
