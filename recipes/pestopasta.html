Classic Pesto Pasta

Classic Pesto Pasta made with basil, pine nuts, garlic, olive oil, and parmesan

Ingredients

  • Basil
  • Pine Nuts
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesan
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook spaghetti
  2. Blend basil, pine nuts, garlic, olive oil, and parmesan
  3. Add pesto to cooked spaghetti
  4. Enjoy!
