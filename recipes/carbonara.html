Classic Carbonara

Classic Carbonara made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Parmesan
  • Pancetta
  • Pepper
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook spaghetti
  2. Whisk eggs and cheese
  3. Fry pancetta
  4. Add cooked spaghetti to pancetta
  5. Add egg mixture
  6. Enjoy!
